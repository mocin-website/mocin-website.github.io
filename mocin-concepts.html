Mocin Concepts

Mocin Concepts is currently only available on social media. There, I post various concepts about redesigns or new products. At present, I do not offer any orders of concepts because I only create concepts of my interest.

Styles

At the moment, I mostly use two-dimensional vector graphic art for my concepts. I create redesigns of famous apps, user interfaces in general and even operating systems.

Software

For the editing part, I usually use Adobe Creative Cloud Software. Therefore, I create my designs and all the layouts with Adobe Illustrator and sometimes Adobe Photoshop.
Animations are mostly done by using Adobe After Effects and to create the full video containing all animation clips, Adobe Premiere Pro is my go-to.
In the future, I plan to use Blender for some 3D animations.

Future plans

My plan is to release redesigns of apps, operating systems and user interfaces frequently on YouTube. In the distant future, I hope to be able to use Blender for creating 3D animations and 3D product concepts. Maybe one day, I will offer creating concepts for potential customers.

Projects
